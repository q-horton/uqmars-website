MARS Newsletter - Week 11

Get ready for an exciting week ahead. We're bringing you a riveting talk on Aerial Manipulation by Dr. Pauline Pounds, a hands-on Path Planning Workshop with UQCS, and a thrilling movie night in collaboration with SMACK and UQFPS.

Kick off the week with Dr. Pounds' eye-opening talk on the future of Aerial Manipulation on Tuesday, 9th May. Don't miss out on the free pizza after the talk! Then, join us on Thursday, 11th May for an interactive Path Planning Workshop where you'll learn about various algorithms and their real-world applications. Python experience is recommended for this workshop.

To wrap up the week, unwind with fellow robotics enthusiasts at our Ghost in the Shell movie night on Tuesday, 16th May. This collaboration with SMACK and UQFPS promises to be a fantastic evening filled with action, science fiction, and camaraderie.

We can't wait to see you all there and witness your enthusiasm for robotics and mechatronics at these exciting events. Let's build, learn, and grow together as a community! See you soon!
