  • Computer Vision Workshop

    Time: TUESDAY, 2 MAY 2023 AT 18:00
    Location: 46-243 - Andrew N. Liveris Building, Collaborative Room
    Type: social event / technical event
    Difficulty: beginner / moderate / advanced
    For week 10 the UQMARS perception specialist, Miguel, has prepared an introductory workshop on computer vision and using OpenCV.

    Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence focusing on enabling computers to identify/process objects and people in images and videos.

    It is used in countless applications of mechatronics such as self driving cars, facial recognition, augmented reality and so much more.

    For this workshop we will be using the OpenCV python library that provides a lot of useful features that make implementing computer vision much easier.

    No background or prior knowledge in CV required, you'll only need yourself, a computer and a webcam to follow along.
